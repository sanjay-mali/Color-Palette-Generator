How Does it Work?

1. Color Scheme Selection

Choose from various color schemes to generate your palette, including Monochromatic, Analogous, Complementary, Split Complementary, and Triadic.

2. Generate Your Palette

Click the “Generate” button to create a fresh set of colors based on your selected scheme. The tool will produce a variety of color options that you can preview and choose from.

3. Interactive Features

Save your favorite palettes for future use, and share them with others using a shareable URL. The colors update in real-time as you interact with the tool.

4. Customizable Output

Adjust and fine-tune your palette to match your needs. Our tool provides the flexibility to create a palette that fits perfectly with your design vision.
